The Board of Directors of TIMEX GROUP INDIA LTD., at its meeting held today have approved the unaudited Consolidated financial results of the company for the period ended March 31, 2021.

For the quarter ended (Q4 FY2021, the company's total income was ₹51.20 crore, against ₹51.47 crore in the corresponding quarter of the previous year (YoY) and ₹53.54 crore in previous quarter of current year (QoQ).

For the quarter ended March 31, 2021(Q4 FY2021), the company's net profit / (loss) was ₹3.98 crore, against ₹-5.59 crore in the corresponding quarter of the previous year (YoY) and ₹4 crore in previous quarter of current year (QoQ).

For the quarter ended March 31, 2021(Q4 FY2021), the company's Earnings Per Share (EPS) was ₹0.18, against ₹-0.77 in the corresponding quarter of the previous year (YoY) and ₹0.18 in previous quarter of current year (QoQ).
